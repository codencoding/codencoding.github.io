About Me

I'm Chase Oden, a recent graduate from UC San Diego with a degree in data science. While at UCSD, I worked as a data science tutor and as a research assistant in a cognitive science research lab. I've been able to hone my teaching and analytical skills through these experiences in ways that wouldn't have been possible through my normal coursework. Since then, I've worked through data scraping, big data analytics, machine learning, model fitting and model tuning.

My interests involve looking at data regarding information about the public as a whole, with my personal interest laying in the field of video games. On the side, I pursue full-stack development so that I can get a complete sense of the programming cycles out there! I'm currently looking for ways to hone my data science skills and really utilize the skills and experience I was able to extract from my years at UCSD.

I graduated from UCSD in December 2020.
